News Sources

People ask where I get my news. This is it: a list of sources I've found to be better curated, less partisan, and more likely to cover things that get missed elsewhere. It's not exhaustive, but it's what I actually read.
National
AP News
Wire service without the editorial layer. Gets the facts down and moves on.
Reuters
International wire with strong business and foreign coverage. Dryer than AP, which is a feature.
Axios
Short format. They respect your time. Good for a quick scan of what matters today.
Local and Regional
Tulsa World
Local paper of record for Tulsa. Worth keeping an eye on even if the site is not great.
Science and Technology
The Verge
Tech coverage that actually engages with why it matters, not just what shipped.
Ars Technica
Goes deeper than most tech outlets. Good on policy, security, and science as well as gadgets.
International
BBC News
Useful for a non-American frame on American events, and for international coverage that US outlets skip.
